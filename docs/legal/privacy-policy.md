# HoopLife Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 13, 2026

HoopLife is a read-only basketball court map. The app is designed to work without an account.

## Information HoopLife Does Not Collect

HoopLife does not require registration and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, payment details, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers.

## Location

If you choose to use the locate button, iOS may ask for location permission. HoopLife uses your location while the app is open to center the map on nearby basketball courts.

Your precise location is processed on your device. HoopLife does not store your location and does not send your location to the HoopLife court database.

You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings.

## Saved Courts

Saved courts are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to a HoopLife account or synced to a server in this release.

## Court Data

HoopLife downloads public court records from its hosted court database. Some court records are imported from OpenStreetMap and may be incomplete until manually checked.

The app displays attribution for OpenStreetMap data. OpenStreetMap data is available under the Open Database License.

## Service and Security Logs

When the app requests court records, hosting providers may process basic network information such as IP address, request time, and error logs for security, abuse prevention, reliability, and delivery of the requested data. HoopLife does not use this information for advertising or tracking.

## Analytics and Advertising

HoopLife does not include third-party advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or behavioral analytics in this release.

## Children

HoopLife is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

If HoopLife adds accounts, submissions, analytics, payments, or other data collection in the future, this policy and the App Store privacy answers should be updated before release.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the HoopLife developer through the support URL listed on the App Store product page.
