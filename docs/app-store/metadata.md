## App Store Description

HoopLife helps you check basketball courts before you leave.

Find nearby courts on a clean map and open practical court facts without creating an account. HoopLife focuses on the details that matter before a run: indoor or outdoor, free or paid, lights, surface, rain impact, nets, rim height, rim type, space, cleanliness, facilities, and access notes.

Some courts are imported from OpenStreetMap and clearly marked when not yet verified by HoopLife. Imported records are useful starting points, and details may be incomplete until manually checked.

HoopLife is built for quick decisions:

- Browse the court map without logging in
- Search courts or areas
- Filter by outdoor, indoor, free, lights, dry surface, nets, and standard rim
- Save courts locally on your device
- Open walking directions in Apple Maps
- See data source and verification status for each court

No ratings. No social feed. Just court facts.

No demo account is required.
