HoopLife

Court facts, not ratings.

HoopLife is a read-only basketball court map that helps players check practical court facts before travelling.

Support Privacy Policy Terms

What HoopLife Shows

Courts, indoor or outdoor status, lighting, surface, rain impact, nets, rim details, facilities, and access notes where available.

No Account Required

The first release is designed for browsing. Saved courts stay on the device and are not synced to a HoopLife account.

Data Sources

Some records are imported from OpenStreetMap and may be incomplete until manually checked by HoopLife.
